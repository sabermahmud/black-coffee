Our Journey

Black Coffee is a trusted coffee brand founded by Shaikh Waleed As Suhaimi with a passion for delivering exceptional coffee and memorable customer experiences. Through dedication to quality and consistency, Black Coffee has expanded to four branches across Al Hanakiyah—Dawar Sa'ah, Aqdah, Souq Al Hanakiyah, and Estitan. We take pride in serving carefully crafted beverages in a warm and welcoming atmosphere where friends, families, and coffee enthusiasts can relax and connect. Every cup reflects our commitment to excellence, making Black Coffee a favorite destination for coffee lovers throughout the region.

Shaikh Waleed As Suhaimy

Founder of BLACK COFFEE
